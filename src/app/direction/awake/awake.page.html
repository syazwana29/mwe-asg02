Awake in the middle of the night

1. Your sleeping environment should be: Dark: Try to make it as dark as you can. Aim for coolness, around 18 to 24 degrees celsius Quiet: If sounds disturb you, white noise could be helpful. Still, silence would be ideal.

2. Exposure to Light Sleeping having the lights on might increase the number of overnight awakenings. In the evening, exposure to light can inhibit the generation of melatonin, which is your body's natural sleep hormone. This may make it more difficult to get to sleep and remain asleep.
